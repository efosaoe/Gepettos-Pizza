About Us

Welcome to Geppetto's Pizza, where passion for great food and family traditions come together in every slice! Since 1986, we've been serving the Sugar Land community with a menu full of delicious, hand-crafted pizzas, pasta, and more. For nearly four decades, our commitment to quality and flavor has made us a local favorite—whether you're dining in, picking up, or having a meal delivered right to your door.

At Geppetto's, we believe in using only the finest ingredients to create the best possible pizza. From our freshly made dough and hand-picked toppings to our secret blend of cheeses and savory sauces, every bite is made with care and love. Our welcoming atmosphere, friendly staff, and family-oriented service make us more than just a pizzeria—we're a place where memories are made, friends gather, and delicious meals are shared.

Whether you're here for a quick lunch, a family dinner, or to celebrate a special occasion, Geppetto's Pizza is proud to be part of the Sugar Land community. Thank you for letting us serve you for all these years, and we look forward to many more!
